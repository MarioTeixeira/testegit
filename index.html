Snake Game

Food eaten: 0

Speed: 50


Key commands:

Press R to restart the game.
Press P to pause or continue the game.
Press + to increase the snake speed.
Press - to decrease the snake speed.
Press A or LEFT to move snake direction to left.
Press W or UP to move the snake direction to up.
Press S or DOWN to move the snake direction to down.
Press D or RIGHT to move the snake direction to right.

Game status: Started, you can pause the game by pressing P.
